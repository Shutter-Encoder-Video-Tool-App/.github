## 🚀 Overview
Shutter Encoder for Windows was created by a video editor who wanted a converter that was powerful yet easy to navigate, and that focus shows. The Shutter Encoder interface is deliberately clean: your source files sit on one side, the output functions on the other, and the settings you need are right where you'd expect them. Under that simple surface is a capable video converter that manages a wide range of formats and codecs, along with tools for cutting clips, adjusting quality, and preparing footage for editing. It's free to use and popular with creators who want professional results without a steep learning curve, making Shutter Encoder a practical everyday tool on a Windows PC.

## ✨ Features
- **Clean, task-focused interface** — the Shutter Encoder interface keeps sources, functions, and settings clearly laid out.
